Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2026

Overview

Scrye is a screen time management app for iOS. We take your privacy seriously. This policy explains what data we collect, how we use it, and your rights.

Data We Collect

Screen time data: Scrye uses Apple's Screen Time API (DeviceActivity framework) to access your device usage information. This data is processed entirely on your device and is never transmitted to our servers.

Usage preferences: Your goals, limits, and app settings are stored locally on your device using Apple's standard storage mechanisms (UserDefaults, SwiftData). This data never leaves your device.

No account required: Scrye does not require you to create an account. We do not collect your name, email address, or any personally identifiable information.

Data We Do Not Collect

We do not collect, store, or transmit: your screen time data, your location, your contacts, your browsing history, your device identifiers, or any analytics about your usage of the app.

Third-Party Services

Scrye does not use any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services. We do not share any data with third parties.

Children's Privacy

Scrye does not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. The app does not require account creation or data submission of any kind.

Changes to This Policy

If we update this policy, we will update the "Last updated" date above. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

Contact

If you have questions about this privacy policy, you can reach us at: privacy@scrye.ca
