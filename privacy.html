Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 16, 2026

About This Policy

Scrye is an iOS app that helps you understand and manage your screen time. This policy describes how the app handles your data. The short version: your data stays on your device. We never see it, collect it, or transmit it anywhere.

What Scrye Accesses

Screen time and app usage data — Scrye uses Apple's DeviceActivity framework (part of Screen Time) to read your device usage. This access is granted through iOS's Family Controls permission, which you must explicitly approve. All processing happens on-device. None of this data is ever sent to external servers, stored in a database, or shared with anyone.

App settings and preferences — Your app settings, limits, and preferences are saved locally on your device using iOS standard storage. This data never leaves your device.

What We Don't Collect

Scrye has no user accounts, no sign-in, and no backend server that receives personal data. We do not collect your name, email address, location, contacts, browsing history, or any device identifiers. There are no analytics SDKs, no advertising networks, and no third-party tracking of any kind embedded in the app.

Third Parties

Scrye does not integrate with any third-party data services. The only external framework used is Apple's own Screen Time API, which operates entirely within iOS and is governed by Apple's privacy policies.

Children

Scrye does not collect personal information from anyone, including children under 13. No account creation or data submission is required to use the app.

Policy Updates

If this policy changes in a meaningful way, we will update the date above and note the change in the App Store release notes. Continued use of the app following an update constitutes acceptance of the revised policy.

Governing Law

This policy is governed by the laws of the Province of Ontario, Canada.
